Nfs

May 3, 2021 1 分钟阅读 nfs

nfs服务

安装

1sudo apt-get install nfs-kernel-server

设置导出

1/home/exports *(rw,nohide,insecure,no_subtree_check,async,no_root_squash)

开启服务

1sudo /etc/init.d/nfs-kernel-server restart

测试

1sudo mount -t nfs -o nolock,vers=3 127.0.0.1:/home/exports /mnt
2ls /mnt
